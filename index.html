Custom IoT Development for Smart Business & Living

Our IoT development company was founded in 2006 in San Francisco, USA as a system integrator and IoT developer. We went on to expand our global footprint and set up an IoT Global center in Eastern Europe to ensure cost-effectiveness and explore new talent pools. Today our Lab is an established team of Iot in Home , Web and embedded system professionals with domain-specific expertise in the Internet of Things development.

MOBILE APPLICATIONS

You can leverage our Lab mobile competence to bring about your IoT device-to-user interaction via apps, including connected control interfaces, mobile data visualization, contactless retail and contextual sensing.

BACK-END INFRASTRUCTURE & INTEGRATION

Our Lab web engineers will employ their tech capabilities to develop an infrastructure behind the front-end part of your connected system, including business logic for automated workflows, data storage and processing, API integration with services to extend the functionality of your solution.

MIDDLEWARE & LOW-LEVEL

To ensure smooth device-to-device or device-to-app data flow, you may need some customization at the low level of your connected system. The Lab is an Internet of Things software development company that has experts in embedded technologies to help you with IoT middleware and low-level application development.
